Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 2025

At Nir & Nitzan Trading Ltd., we value your privacy and are committed to protecting it. Our games are designed for entertainment purposes and are completely safe for all users. Below is a detailed explanation of how we handle user privacy:

  • No data collection: We do not collect, store, or share any personal information, such as names, emails, device identifiers, or locations.
